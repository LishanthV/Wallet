How it works

Every time you pay via Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, or your bank's UPI, a transaction alert email is sent to your Gmail. We scan those emails, extract the amount & merchant, then let you review & confirm before adding to your expenses. Nothing is saved without your approval.
